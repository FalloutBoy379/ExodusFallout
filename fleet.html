THE FLEET

The ExoShip Fleet consist of various types of space transport like Passenger Vessels, Cargo Vessels and Military Ships.
Most of the vessels are used for inter-colony travel within Exodus territory.
However, military vessels are often fitted with features to operate outside the safety of Exodus.

Elon

The first colony space transport, made back in 2017. Since then , three other sister ships have been made.

  • Capacity: 2,00,500 people
  • Top Speed: 0.001 Light Year/Year

Bond

Bond is a high-end passenger ship which offers cruises to civilians. However, this class of ships cannot be used for carrying passenger cargo.

  • Capacity: 1000 people
  • Top Speed: 0.0008 Light Year/Year

Vader

Vader was one of the first ships to arrive at Exodus. Along with building material, the ship brought the belongings of the people onboard the "Elon".

  • Capacity: 18,000 Tons
  • Top Speed: 0.0034 Light Year/Year

Indiana

Indiana is a supply ship which is used to refuel transporters without visiting a port. It also helps the ESF(Exodus Space Force) in certain missions.

  • Capacity: 22,000 Tons
  • Tope Speed: 0.0012 Light Year/Year
