About

ExoShips was founded in 2012 when the Scientific Communities of Earth had expressed the need for the private sector to join the race towards making space transports capable of evacuating areas quickly.
The first vessel made was a shuttle to carry the citizens to and from the main vessel, which was an Elon Class Vessel at the time.

After developing a few dozen prototypes, ExoShips Engineers hit the mark. A new engine had been made which would be capable of helping humans colonize the universe.

5 years after the establishment of the company, the apocalypse arrived upon the planet. The spacecraft, controlled and maintained by it's crew of 940 people, managed to save 40% of the world's population by making repeated journeys from the Exodus habitats to Earth over a few days.
